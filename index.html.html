FAQ

Common questions.

Does the AI really sound human? ⌄
Yes. We use Retell AI — the leading conversational voice platform — which produces natural, low-latency speech with realistic tone and rhythm. Most callers don't realise they're speaking with an AI. You can hear a live example on the demo call.
What if a call is too complex for the AI? ⌄
The AI is trained to recognise when a call needs a human. It can take a detailed message, offer a callback, or transfer to you directly — depending on how you configure it. Nothing falls through the cracks.
Do I need to be technical? ⌄
Not at all. We handle 100% of the setup. You'll have a simple dashboard to view bookings and call summaries — no code, no complex systems to log in to unless you want them.
How long does setup actually take? ⌄
48 hours from kickoff call to live system. This is a hard commitment we make to every client. Setup includes voice AI configuration, CRM, calendar integration, and automation workflows — all done by us.
Can I cancel the monthly retainer? ⌄
Yes. Month-to-month, no lock-in contract. We keep clients through results, not cancellation penalties. If the system isn't performing, we fix it — and if you still want to leave, you can with 30 days notice.
What countries do you work with? ⌄
We primarily serve businesses in the US, Australia, Canada, and New Zealand. If you're elsewhere, reach out — we assess each enquiry individually and may be able to accommodate you.
